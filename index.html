About

Great software isn't just about code; it's about people. I bridge the gap between development and user experience, ensuring products are intuitive for end users and its developers.

My journey into software development began with an enjoyment for problem-solving. While working as a data analyst, I enjoyed the technical aspects - data wrangling, Python scripting, and automation - but I soon realized my true passion lay in building software. I self-taught coding through open-source resources like The Odin Project, before formally strengthening my skills by completing a software development program at the University of British Columbia (UBC).

My background in product management has given me a strong foundation in Agile methodologies, cross-functional collaboration, and gathering user feedback to drive continuous, iterative product improvements. I've worked closely with developers, business leaders, and end users to ensure that technical solutions align with real-world needs.

Today, I focus on web development, app development, and software engineering, applying both my technical and product expertise to create scalable, user-centric solutions.
